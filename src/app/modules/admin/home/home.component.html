How it works?
Here is the explanation of how CryptoArk works
Start using CryptoArk In order to start using CryptoArk, you need to register as a buyer or seller. As soon as you are registered, you will already have unlocked the functions of buying or selling products. To access them in the vendors menu you can use the top button to register a new product. In case you want to buy, you must also access the vendors menu to search for the desired vendor. When you enter the seller's detail, you will be able to see their products for sale. Products The products contain 3 levels of information, each of them has a price set by the seller. Initially you have access to a first description of the product for free. If you want more information about it, you must buy product information, first level 1 and then the following. Finally the seller will reveal contact information for the purchase of the product. How to buy To buy products, you must access the sellers menu. Find the right seller and access his detail, there you can find all his products for sale. If you are interested in one of them, you must click on buy and you must pay the price of the first level. After the seller must decrypt the information personally, only the buyer can have access to said information. If the seller does not decrypt the information within 30 days, the buyer can withdraw the amount paid for that level. If the seller decrypts the information, the buyer can buy the next level. In the same way, the seller will have to decrypt the information, ... For the purchase of levels 2 and 3, it will be accessed from the menu of purchased products. There you will also find the details of the products purchased. How to sell To sell products you must access the sellers menu, where you will find a button to register products. To do this you must follow the steps for your registration. When someone buys one of your products, said product will appear in the menu of purchased products so that you can reveal the information paid.
